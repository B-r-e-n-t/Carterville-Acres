Forged in Nature: A Weekend Art Retreat

Connect with your inner fire and leave with beautiful jewelry and art pieces of your own creation.

About This Retreat

Spend a weekend immersed in creativity and nature. Whether you're a practiced artist or a curious beginner, this retreat offers a welcoming environment to create, explore, and unwind among the forests and ponds of Carterville Acres. This two-day, one night retreat includes meals, hands-on workshops led by local artists Kelly Diemond and Marissa Perkins, and fireside conversations in the evenings.

Schedule Snapshot

Saturday

Arrival, time to hike, kayak, or explore the grounds, a meditative art journaling activity, and evening fire circle. Snacks and dinner provided.

Sunday

Sunrise walk to the beach, Jewelry-making session, mixed-media art session, and departure. Breakfast and lunch provided. Those interested in staying an additional night will received a reduced rate for cabins.

What's Included

  • 2 nights in a shared cabin (private cabins available at extra cost)
  • Three art workshops: mixed media & jewelry making
  • All supplies provided.
  • Snacks and meals provided.
  • Access to all campground amenities: trails, beach, bonfire, and kayaks available for rent.

What to Bring

  • Clothing for outdoor activities
  • Art journal (optional)
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Flashlight or headlamp
  • Final packing list will be shared after registration closes.

About the Instructor

Marissa Perkins

Marissa is a jewelry artist and maker based in upstate New York. Her work is inspired by and a summons to strong, adventurous women who love the outdoors. Her distinctive jewelry designs are inspired by nature and Marissa's love of the wild.

Kelly Diemond

Kelly is a jewelry artist and instructor based in central New York. Her studio classes allow practicing hobbyists and curious crafters to experiment with metals and craft their own custom pieces. She has found her true calling in teaching, where she's hooked on seeing other budding artists find their joy,

FAQs

Are beginners welcome?

Yes! Prior art or jewelry making experience is not needed.

Can I bring my own art supplies?

Yes — we encourage you to bring any art journaling materials you love, as there will be opportunities for community crafting in the lodge. All required supplies for the scheduled sessions will be provided.

What is the cancellation policy?

Registrants can receive a 50% refund if they cancel 30 or more days in advance. No refunds within 29 days of event.

What meals are included?

Saturday snacks, dinner, and s'mores. Sunday breakfast and lunch. Vegetarian options will be avaialable; meal preferences and dietary restrictions will be collected from participants in the registration form.
