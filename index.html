Welcome to the Campground Where Everyone Belongs.

At Carterville Acres, we believe the outdoors should be for everyone. That’s why we’ve designed a campground where people of all backgrounds, experiences, and identities can come together to create memories under the stars. Whether you’re an experienced camper, a family trying it out for the first time, or someone looking for a getaway without the gear, we’ve got you covered.

From fully-equipped cabins to cozy tent sites, we’ve thought of every detail to make your stay as welcoming as possible. Our team is here to help you connect with nature and with each other in a space that believes in camping for all.
